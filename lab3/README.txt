########################### LAB 3 README ###########################

To run this lab simply open index.html in a browser (I developed it in Chrome).
To move the camera you use:

    a: Move camera left
    d: Move camera right
    s: Move camera down
    w: Move camera up
    x: Rotate camera on z-axis clockwise
    z: Rotate camera on z-axis counter-clockwise

To move the robot use:

    <left arrow>:       spin left
    <right arrow>:      spin right
    <down arrow>:       spin around
    <up arrow>:         move forward
    <enter>:            do dance move 1
    shift + <enter>:    do dance move 1
